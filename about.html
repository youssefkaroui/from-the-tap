Our Mission

Get lit, Get Woke
Need to something more than that Corona? Getting bored of that budweiser? Get your quench quenched with the quenchiest of beers near you and switch it up with some of your local favorites! With our app From The Tap you can look up local breweries in your area and find your next go to drink! And then when you do, click that button below to entertain yourself with some fun facts while you get your buzz on! That way you can get your mind blown as well as your liver! Our team's passion for beer has brought us together to create From The Tap, a simple yet exciting way to get your buzz on! From The Tap's goal is to bring community together through drinking and supporting our small local businesses at the same time. by purchasing from your local breweries, you can find more unique and flavorful beverages than what your local liquor store can offer! Try our app today and let get lit!
